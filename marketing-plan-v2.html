01
Foundation · Step One

Store Performance & SEO

Search Visibility

Before any marketing works, your store must be technically ready and visible on Google. Without a strong foundation, every dollar you spend on ads and promotions is wasted. This step builds the bedrock.

What We Do In This Step
🔑
Products SEO Optimization Optimise every product title, meta description, URL slug, and alt text with high-converting keywords so Google sends shoppers directly to your products.
🔗
On-page & Off-page Optimization Improve internal linking, page load speed, mobile responsiveness, structured data, and build quality backlinks that signal authority to Google.
🗺️
Google Sitemap & Search Console Setup Submit your sitemap to Google, connect Search Console, fix crawl errors, and track exactly how your store performs in search results every day.
Why This Step Is Critical

Organic SEO traffic is 100% free and compounds over time. A store with strong SEO keeps getting visitors without paying for each click — it's the only marketing channel that truly pays you back long-term. Stores with optimised SEO typically see 40–200% more organic traffic within 90 days.

GlowSkin was getting 120 monthly organic visitors. After optimising all 48 product pages and setting up Search Console, their titles changed from "Face Cream 50ml" to "Hydrating Vitamin C Face Cream for Sensitive Skin." Google began indexing them for 60+ new keywords.

↑ 340% organic traffic in 90 days Page 1 for 12 keywords +$4,200/mo revenue from SEO

Retention · Step Two

Klaviyo Email Marketing

Email marketing generates an average $42 for every $1 spent — the highest ROI of any marketing channel. Klaviyo plugs directly into Shopify and turns every customer action into an automated revenue opportunity.

What We Do In This Step
🔌
Shopify + Klaviyo Integration Sync your full product catalogue, order history, and customer segments into Klaviyo so every email is personalised and perfectly timed.
⚡
Email Marketing Flows Design Build high-converting automated flows: Welcome Series, Abandoned Cart (3-email), Browse Abandonment, Post-Purchase, Win-Back, and VIP sequences.
🪟
Attractive Pop-Up Forms Design exit-intent and timed popups that offer incentives (10% off, free shipping) to capture emails — growing your list on autopilot 24/7.
📢
Email Campaigns Send regular promotional campaigns: product launches, seasonal sales, and newsletters that keep your audience engaged and coming back to buy.
🤖
Full Email Automation Set up behavioural triggers so the right email reaches the right customer at exactly the right moment — without you lifting a finger.
Why This Step Is Critical

An abandoned cart sequence alone typically recovers 8–15% of lost sales. On a store with $50K monthly revenue, that's $4,000–$7,500 per month in revenue you'd otherwise never see — fully automated. Email is the only channel where you own your audience completely.

🔗
Integration with Shopify Connect your Shopify store to all social platforms so you can tag products directly in posts — turning every post into a shoppable experience.

Stunning Content Creation Produce scroll-stopping visuals, Reels, product videos, and carousel posts using proven content frameworks that generate saves, shares, and clicks.

Google & Social Media Shops

Get your products listed inside the platforms where 4.9 billion people shop every day — completely free. Google Shopping, Facebook Shop, Instagram Shop, Pinterest Catalog, and TikTok Shop remove the friction between discovery and purchase.

What We Do In This Step

Google Merchant Center Upload your full product catalogue to Google Merchant Center so your products appear in Google Shopping tabs, Google Images, and Search — capturing high-intent buyers actively searching for what you sell.

Facebook Shop Build a complete Facebook Shop with collections and product tags so your 2.9 billion potential buyers can browse and purchase without leaving Facebook.

Pinterest Catalog Sync your Shopify catalogue to Pinterest — the platform where 85% of users make purchases after saving a product pin. Perfect for home, fashion, beauty, and lifestyle brands.
